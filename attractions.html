Top Attractions

Prizren Fortress

(Dino, 2023)

Overlooking the city, Prizren Fortress offers spectacular views and centuries of history dating back to medieval times.

Stone Bridge

(Barnes, 2025)

The famous Stone Bridge crosses the Lumbardhi River and is one of Prizren's most photographed landmarks.

Sinan Pasha Mosque

(Sinani, 2024)

Built in the 17th century, this mosque is one of the city's finest examples of Ottoman architecture.
